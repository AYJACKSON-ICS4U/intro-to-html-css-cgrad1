Well Done Hamburgers

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Beef Patties
Bread Buns
Season Salt
Cheese 		12
12

Any

Step 1: Lay the patties on the grill.
Step 2: Flip the patties every 3-4 minutes until both sides are sufficiently cooked.
Step 3: When patties are about halfway done, shake seasoning salt on both sides lightly.
Step 4: For cheeseburgers, lay the cheese onto one side about 3/4 the way done so it doesn't ruin your grill.
Step 5: Put the finished burgers onto buns and serve them with toppings and condiments.

Shrimp Scampi

  • Time: 40m
  • Servings: 8

Step 1: Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
Step 2: Arrange shrimp in a single layer in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Evenly pour olive oil over shrimp and top with bread crumbs, parsley, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cover dish with aluminum foil.
Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until shrimp are cooked through, about 20 minutes.
Step 4: Combine butter, white wine, and hot sauce in a small bowl; pour over shrimp. Top shrimp with Parmesan-Romano cheese.
Step 5: Bake in the oven until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes more.
